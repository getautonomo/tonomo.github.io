John visits Aerial Canvas in San Francisco and works alongside their team to sketch a solution for a fast-growing industry that didn't yet have software built for it.

Off the clock, Feliks is usually outdoors — hiking, snowboarding, traveling, or riding his electric unicycle around the city.
